Our Story

2018

Founded in Istanbul

LuxeStay launched with 50 handpicked hotels across Turkey, built by a small team of hospitality professionals who wanted to change how people book luxury travel.

2020

Expanded to 10 Countries

Despite a challenging year for travel, we grew our portfolio to cover the Middle East, Greece, and Spain, onboarding over 300 properties.

2022

Launched LuxeStay Rewards

Our loyalty program launched to global acclaim, with over 100,000 members signing up within the first three months and earning points on every booking.

2024

1,000+ Hotels Milestone

We crossed 1,000 curated properties across 35 countries, serving half a million guests and earning recognition as a top luxury booking platform.

2026

Global Expansion

Reaching 40 countries and 1,200 properties, LuxeStay continues to set the standard for curated luxury travel worldwide.
